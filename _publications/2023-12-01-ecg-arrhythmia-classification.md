This research focuses on the development of a robust deep learning architecture for the automated detection of cardiac arrhythmias.

### Key Contributions:
* **Architecture:** Implementation of a CNN/ResNet-based model for high-precision signal classification.
* **Performance:** Achieved an accuracy rate of **99.32%** in experimental validations.
* **Impact:** The study provides a scalable solution for AI-driven clinical diagnostics, reducing the margin of error in ECG interpretation.

Published in the **IEEE Xplore Digital Library**.
