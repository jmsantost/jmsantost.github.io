This research focuses on the development of a robust deep learning architecture for the automated detection of cardiac arrhythmias. Using a CNN/ResNet-based model, the study achieved a high-precision classification rate of 99.32%.

### Key Contributions:
* **Architecture Implementation**: Development of specialized neural network layers for ECG signal processing.
* **High Accuracy**: Demonstrated a 99.32% success rate in detecting various types of arrhythmias.
* **Clinical Application**: Provides a scalable AI-driven solution for clinical diagnostics in cardiology.
